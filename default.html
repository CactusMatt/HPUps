Our Purpose

To enable students, science majors and non-majors alike, to develop a scientific literacy, through:
  • Appreciation of the contributions of science to modern life.
  • Development of an awareness of measures necessary for personal safety and ecological responsibility in this scientific era; and
  • Development of a background of information in the physical science discipline studied.

Chemistry Degree Curriculum

The department of Physical Sciences offers a curriculum such that graduates with a major in Chemistry will:
  • Understand, effectively communicate, and practice the basic theories of Chemistry.
  • Obtain positions as scientists, educators, and/or obtain admission to a graduate or professional school of their choice.
  • Will be prepared to compete in their chosen field.

Forensics Degree Curriculum

The Department of Physical Sciences offers a curriculum such that graduates with a major in Forensic Science will:
  • Understand, communicate effectively and engage thoughtfully in the practice of forensic science.
  • Obtain positions as laboratory scientists or investigators or procure admission to a graduate program.
  • Compete ably in either of the above avenues.
The Physical Science Department is located within the Winebrenner Memorial Hall of Science upon the campus of Howard Payne University.
